Introduction

The SmartIG application, developed for the FAE-R project at RWTH, is designed to enhance living and working space efficiency and comfort. Its key features include:

  1. Utilizes advanced wireless sensor technology to monitor environmental parameters like humidity and CO2 levels.
  2. Offers a user-centric control system for efficient heating based on user preferences and behavior patterns.
  3. Incorporates self-learning capabilities for automatic adjustments, alongside manual remote temperature control and heating set-point adjustments via the application.
  4. Ensures compatibility with existing heating systems in interim buildings, easy installation, and minimal maintenance needs.
  5. Enhances user experience in working environments and contributes to energy efficiency, thus promoting a greener world.

Basic Features

SMARTIG offers various control modes to maintain the office temperature ideal. Furthermore, users can monitor the environmental parameters of their office.

Setting Control Mode

Navigate to the "Home" tab. Here, you can switch between various control modes, enabling you to dictate how the system responds to environmental changes in real-time.

OFF Mode

The control system is turned off and does not interact with sensors. This mode is suitable for times when you are away for several days, such as vacations or holidays.

Manual Mode

In Manual mode, you can set the temperature to a specific degree for a short period. The room's temperature setpoint will remain at this level until either the control mode or setpoint changes.

Adaptive Mode

Adaptive mode automatically sets the room temperature when it detects someone in the room based on sensor data. It adjusts the temperature according to calibrated comfort model and previous feedback and setpoints.

Monitoring

The monitoring feature provides real-time feedback on room conditions. Check CO2, temperature, humidity, and other essential parameters, ensuring your environment remains optimal.

Advanced Features

The advanced features of SmartIG elevate the user experience by learning from user feedback on office temperature preferences and adjusting settings over time for tailored comfort.

Additionally, the app enhances user convenience by providing notifications about room conditions, keeping users informed and in control, even when they're on the move. The geofencing feature adds another layer of automation, enabling the app to adjust the office environment as the user approaches or leaves the area.

It is important to note that while the feedback learning capability is available across all platforms, the notification and geofencing features are exclusive to the iOS and Android applications, and not included in the web app version.

Thermal Feedback

Our thermal feedback mechanism learns from your preferences. Over time, it adjusts settings to align with your comfort patterns, making the system more intuitive with each interaction.

Notification

Stay informed with real-time notifications. Whether it's a change in office conditions or system updates, our app ensures you're always in the know. You can easily activate notification via settings section in application.

Geo-fencing

With geo-fencing, your office's temperature automation responds to your location. As you approach to the office, the system might adjust settings, preparing the perfect environment for your arrival. You can easily activate geo-fencing feature via settings section in application.
